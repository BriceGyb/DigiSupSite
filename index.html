DontWaste: Award-Winning Platform for Reducing Food Waste

We’re proud to share that DontWaste was recently awarded in Casablanca, Morocco

for its innovative approach in helping merchants sell nearly expired food items at reduced prices. This recognition solidifies our commitment to reducing food waste and supporting local businesses. Our platform connects consumers with stores and restaurants that offer quality food nearing expiration, ensuring it doesn’t go to waste while benefiting both the community and the environment.

  • 🔸 Empowering local merchants to reduce food waste

  • 🔸 Sustainable solutions for a better future

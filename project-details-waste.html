Project DontWaste

DontWaste - Reducing Food Waste, Promoting Sustainability

DontWaste is our innovative platform that connects vendors with consumers seeking nearly expired food products, promoting sustainable consumption and reducing food waste. By enabling quick access to affordable groceries, DontWaste helps vendors clear inventory while providing cost-effective options for consumers.

Award Recognition

DontWaste was honored with the 3rd Place Innovation Award at the prestigious event in Casablanca, Morocco. This recognition celebrates our commitment to reducing food waste and promoting sustainable consumption by connecting vendors with consumers for nearly expired products. The award highlights DontWaste’s impact and potential to drive positive change in food distribution across the region.

  • Reduces food waste by connecting vendors with nearly expired products to consumers

  • Offers affordable options for consumers while helping vendors clear unsold inventory

  • Promotes sustainable consumption and supports local communities
